Artistic Approach

When I look at the sky a summer night, I know half stars I see doesn't exist anymore. So I've the choice to make some to exist and to change their next day. Know the Truth is beyond my perception's ability. The conditioned look gives the sense and the relative truth of what is seen.


Failing to gain access to the absolute, the necessary acceptance of this truths' relativity is the main element I try to express through my painting.


First facing the will to transcend the childhood's traumas, my first paintings, which show the despair and sadness, suggest detachment and serenity as if to say "look, beautiful is possible."


The detachment and hope to try to gain the absolute to escape the dual and contradictory vision of the truths of the world is my inspiration. This research is omnipresent in my paintings, sometimes expressed with violence usually only suggested to mean certainties' stupidity.


A research I chose to recreate in my painting technique through the juxtaposition of elements contradictory and opposite in appearance, like black and white, mineral and living, violent of expressions and the soft of shapes, work with a knife and brush.


The use of acrylic, forcing the timeliness, promotes spontaneous expression of emotions. They will materialize on the canvas and form the background from which will develop the final representation of this duality.
